# Our Story — Anniversary Gift

A personal, interactive anniversary page you can send to her.

## Quick send (today)

1. **Personalize** — Open `index.html` in any text editor and edit the `CONFIG` section at the bottom (names, letter, timeline, reasons).
2. **Add photos** (optional but magical):
   - Create a folder named `photos`
   - Add `1.jpg`, `2.jpg`, `3.jpg`, `4.jpg` (or change paths in `CONFIG.photos`)
3. **Add music** (optional): drop a file named `music.mp3` next to `index.html` (soft instrumental works best).
4. **Send it:**
   - **iPhone/Mac:** AirDrop the whole `Our Story` folder, or zip it first.
   - **Link:** Drag the folder to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) — you get a URL to text her.
   - **Open locally:** Double-click `index.html` (works best if photos/music are in the folder).

## What she'll experience

- Opening animation → “Open your gift”
- Tap-to-open **envelope** with your love letter
- **Timeline** of your story (edit the moments)
- **Flip cards** — reasons you adore her
- **Photo gallery** (or elegant placeholders until you add pics)
- **Celebrate us** button — heart shower finale

## Tips

- Replace placeholder timeline text with real dates and memories — that's what makes her cry (the good way).
- Use square or landscape photos; the layout adapts automatically.
- Preview on your phone before sending — scroll through once as she will.

Happy anniversary.
